What is ADI?

The Analytical Democratic Integrity (ADI) algorithm measures and tracks the strength of democratic structures across nations. By combining advanced AI models with validated data sources, including the FreedomHouse Index, ADI provides a clear, actionable score that reflects political freedoms, civil liberties, and government transparency.

Key Metrics

  • Integrity Index: A dynamic score reflecting democratic health and resilience.
  • Stability Signals: Predictive indicators for early warnings of political decline.
  • Cross-Correlation: Data aligned with global indices like FreedomHouse and World Bank governance indicators.

Use Cases

ADI serves as a powerful tool for:

  • Researchers and policy analysts tracking democratic trends.
  • Journalists investigating shifts in governance and freedoms.
  • Non-profits and NGOs advocating for civil liberties and justice.
